Before We Begin

Your age helps distinguish your true Prakriti (fixed constitution) from Kala Svabhava (age-phase dosha) — a critical distinction in classical Ayurveda.

THREE LIFE PHASES — CS SUTRASTHANA 7.5-8 | AH SUTRASTHANA 1.9
🌊 Balya (0–20 yrs): Kapha dominates — growth phase, tissue building
🔥 Madhyama (20–60 yrs): Pitta dominates — ideal Prakriti assessment window
🌬️ Vriddha (60+ yrs): Vata increases — depletion phase
Prakriti is FIXED at conception (CS Sharirasthana 4.36: "Yā prakṛtiḥ sā sthirā bhavati"). Kala Svabhava is the natural age-phase dosha that overlaps with it.
Please enter a valid age between 16 and 80.
